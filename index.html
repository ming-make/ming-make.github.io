Research

I am a first-year master student at Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Huawei Li and Prof. Shengwen Liang.

My research interests lie in computer architecture, with a focus on but not limited to hardware acceleration and memory system.

  • Accelerate large language model by leveraging in-memory computing techniques.
  • Automated chip design by domain-specific LLM.
  • Develop new tools to make EDA more efficient.
